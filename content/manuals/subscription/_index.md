Docker subscriptions provide licensing and usage entitlements for Docker
products. After [creating your account](/manuals/accounts/create-account.md),
you can purchase several subscription types that extend your Docker product
usage.

## Docker plans and add-ons

Docker subscriptions can be categorized into plans and add-ons.

- Plans come with built-in quotas with set usage limits.
- Add-ons extend those limits when you purchase seats, licenses, repositories, or minutes.

For product-specific details on quotas and available add-ons, see the relevant
product page.
