Docker subscriptions provide licensing and usage entitlements for Docker
products. Each subscription covers a specific product or capability. Select
the one you want to learn about or manage from the shelf below.

## Purchase Docker products

All purchase actions flow through the product catalog in
Docker Home. To purchase any Docker subscription:

1. Sign in to
   [Docker Home](https://app.docker.com/) and go to **Billing**.
1. Select **Browse products**.
