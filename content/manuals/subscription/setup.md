In Billing, you can purchase new subscriptions from the Product catalog and
manage existing ones from Plan overview. The Product catalog lists all
self-serve Docker products, while Plan overview shows your active plans and
lets you adjust or cancel them.

## Purchase new subscription

You can purchase Docker products through the product catalog:

1. Sign in to [Docker Home](https://app.docker.com/), then choose your personal
   account or your organization account.
1. Go to **Billing**.
   - The **Billing** overview page lets you track your active plans.
   - To learn more about billing, see [Billing](/manuals/billing/_index.md).
1. Select **Browse products**.
   - The **Products** page is Docker's product catalog.
   - It lists all products you can purchase via self-serve.
1. Purchase a product by selecting **View plans**.
1. Verify your billing details, continue to payment, and complete checkout.

### Purchase sales-led products

Some products specify that they're sales-led. You must
[contact sales](https://www.docker.com/pricing/contact-sales/) to opt in to
these products.
