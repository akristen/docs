Gordon plans increase your maximum Gordon usage allowance on a personal Docker
account. Three tiers are available — Plus, Max, and Ultra — each providing a
higher allowance. Gordon plans are billed monthly at the first of the month,
with prorated charges if you purchase mid-month.

To purchase, sign in to [Docker Home](https://app.docker.com/), go to
**Billing**, select **Browse products**, then choose **View plans** from the
**Upgrade Gordon** section.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Gordon plans apply to personal Docker accounts only. If you purchase a Gordon
> plan while signed in with an organization account, the subscription applies
> to your personal account automatically.

## Usage

Each Gordon plan tier grants a higher monthly usage allowance for your personal
account. Usage is tracked per calendar month and resets at the start of each
new month.

For details on usage limits per tier, see
[Gordon usage limits and tiers](/manuals/ai/gordon/usage-limits.md).

## Upgrade plan

To add a new Gordon plan to your account:

1. Sign in to [Docker Home](https://app.docker.com/) and select your personal
   account.
1. Select **Billing**, then **Browse products**.
1. Select **View plans** from the **Upgrade Gordon** section of the products
   page.
1. Choose the Gordon plan you want.
1. Verify your billing details, continue to payment, and complete checkout.

To upgrade from an active Gordon plan to a higher tier:

1. From the Billing overview page, select **Manage** from the Gordon tile under
   your active plans.
1. Select **Upgrade plan** and choose the new tier.
1. Verify your billing details, continue to payment, and complete checkout.

Your new usage allowance takes effect immediately. You pay a prorated charge
for the higher tier for the rest of the current billing period.

## Deactivate

To cancel your Gordon plan:

1. From the Billing overview page, select **Manage** from the Gordon tile under
   your active plans.
1. Select **Cancel plan** or **Disable auto-renewal**.

Your allowance reverts to the included amount in your Docker Individual
subscription at the end of the current billing cycle.
